Things Students want to know!




1. Am I in the right room?


I will have programming related posters and signs in the word wall. I will also be in front of the door to greet students and ensure that they entered the right classroom or guide them towards the right classrooms.




2. Where am I supposed to sit?


Students can select their seat and will be sitting there for the rest of the semester.




3. What are the rules in this classroom?


Participate in class activities. Be respectful. Talk in a lower voice. Stay seated. Be on time.




4. What will I be doing this year?


You will be learning and writing codes in C++. You will be completing the daily objectives written on board. You will be working individually and with a group. You will be completing assignments, tests, and exit tickets.




5. How will I be graded?


You will be graded based on your effort and performance. The grading criteria will be posted on the syllabus




6. Who is the teacher as a person?


I am Nahian. English is my 5th language. I like to sleep and play games. I make video games, websites, and softwares. I am a Rice university student.




7. Will the teacher treat me as a human being?


Everyone will be treated with respect, dignity, and fairly.
